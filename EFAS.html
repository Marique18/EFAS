Eijnden Financial & Advisory Services B.V. is owned by Jean-Rene van den Eijnden. Jean-Rene is also the person who renders services to the customers of Eijnden Financial & Advisory Services B.V.

Jean-Rene van den Eijnden is a Dutch national living in the Netherlands. He is a Dutch chartered accountant and has a degree in business administration from the Free University in Amsterdam. After graduation he worked for 14 years in the international practice of Ernst & Young Amsterdam. After which, he worked for 10 years with the international royalty processing center of Universal Music (formerly PolyGram, a division of Philips Electronics). He joined the Mitsui group in 2005 as group controller fot their European automotive division where he worked until 2010. Thereafter he worked for the Mitsui group for their finance and holding companies in the Netherlands for 3 year. During the period 2014-2020 he worked as CFO for the European railway leasing company of the Mitsui group. He is currently also a parttime managing director of Minejesa Power B.V., next to his activities as managing director of Eijnden Financial & Advisory Services B.V.
